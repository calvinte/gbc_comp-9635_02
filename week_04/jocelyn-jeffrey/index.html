About Us

Mykonos Restaurant, located just one block north of Davenport on Yonge Street, serves traditional Greek and Mediterranean cuisine, perfect a business lunch or casual dinner. Specialties include Santorini chicken, lamb shank , grilled Atlantic salmon and salads. The extensive menu also includes less traditional items, like pizza and pasta.

It may not bring to mind windmills or cool sea breezes but the menu will transport you in an air reminiscent of the lovely Greek island for which it is named.
